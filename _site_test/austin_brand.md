The City of Austin’s brand reflects the spirit of our community, capturing its vibrant energy, authentic character, and our commitment to service.

﻿

These guidelines define our verbal and visual identity, offering clear direction for communicating the City of Austin brand with consistency, clarity, and heart. With practical examples and adaptable standards, this guide supports City employees and partners in representing Austin across all channels while allowing space for expression and spontaneity.
